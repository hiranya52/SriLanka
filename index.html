Where is Sri Lanka?

Sri Lanka, often called "The Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a tropical island nation located south of India, just above the equator. Nestled in the Indian Ocean, it’s part of South Asia and enjoys a strategic position along major maritime trade routes.
